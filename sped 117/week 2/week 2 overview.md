Issue Brief:

This class includes an independent project called an Issue Brief. Please visit the Issue Brief Module on Canvas to get details about this assignment. Then, there is an assignment in the Week 2 Module that prompts you to identify a topic. Please take advantage of this opportunity for feedback from your Team Leader!



Module 2 Topics:

The History of Disabled People in America



From Campus Life to Independent Living



The Center for Independent Living



Willowbrook Exposed



The Willowbrook Lawsuit



Module 2 Objectives:

Students will review a brief history of persons with disabilities in the U.S. from the 1700s to 1970.



Students will read about Ed Roberts as a crucial disability rights activist in the Independent Living Movement.



Students will read about Judy Heumann as an integral disability rights activist with a beginning role in the Center for Independent Living (CIL)



Students will learn about Willowbrook and the political action and legislation which followed the Willowbrook lawsuit.
