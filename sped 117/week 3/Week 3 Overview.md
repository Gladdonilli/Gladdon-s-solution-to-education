Module 3 Topics:

Laws that protect the rights of people with disabilities

Employment for people with disabilities

Hiring people with disabilities

Employment barriers and solutions for people with intellectual disabilities

An Important Court Case that Affected People with Disabilities' Right to Independent Living: The Olmstead Case 

Module 3 Objectives:

Students will understand and recognize laws that afford protections to people with disabilities.

Students will learn about employment for people with disabilities and be able to analyze information regarding employment barriers and solutions.

Students will understand employment barriers and solutions for people with intellectual disabilities.

Students will learn how the societal push for inclusion affects employment opportunities.

Students will recognize the importance of independent living for people with disabilities.

Students will read about the Olmstead supreme court decision that set precedence for persons with disabilities to      have access to community living programs.

Students will identify what support is needed in order for a person with a disability to live independently.
